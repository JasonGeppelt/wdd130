Our Perfect Dates!

White Water Rafting

Our classic adventure date! Enjoy the thrill of white water rafting with your special date. After your raft trip, take a look at your action-packed set of photos, enjoy a snack from our caf√©, and sip a refreshing beverage while you relax with your date in our lounge.

Kayaking

Want to go out on the water but the noise and ferocity of rafting might take away from your date? Our kayaking date is a great change to explore the river and take in the peaceful nature of your surroundings.

Bike Rides

With both beginner and intermediate bike paths, we have options for everyone to have a wonderful time biking down a beautiful forest trail. The way back up can be difficult for some, so we provide a state-of-the-art gondala to carry you back up the hill.

Campfires

Special moments are made every night around our campfires. Work with your date to build your own fire, or plan a group date for fun with friends! You can bring your own food, or pick up complimentary s'mores supplies at our lounge nearby.

Nature Hikes

An easy, scenic path through our neighboring forest area is always readily maintained. A season pass will get you free entrance all season long to bring as many dates, or the same date, to experience this nature hike again and again.

Rope Courses

A personal favorite of many of our staff, the rope courses we provide are a test of your enginuity and skill! Come brave the towering heights of our toughest courses and work with your date to overcome obstacles.
