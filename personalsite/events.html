Events and Locations

Represented by the three regions of Idaho, the Dog-Eat-Dog official hot dog annual competition is hosted at the State Fair every year. Along with that main event, there are many qualifying contests throughout the year in each of the regions.

Please join us at the locations and times below!

State Grand Championship

Time: 2:00pm | Location: Idaho State Fair

BYUI Qualifiers

Time: 3:00pm | Location: BYUI Campus

Boise Qualifiers

Time: 4:00pm | Location: Boise Best Buy

Jackson Hole Qualifiers

Time: 6:00pm | Location: Jackson Hole

Idaho Falls Qualifiers

Time: 2:00pm | Location: I.F. Courthouse

Twin Falls Qualifiers

Time: 7:00pm | Location: Twin Falls

Pocatello Qualifiers

Time: 5:00pm | Location: Pocatello High

Sign ups will be provited at each Qualifier Event. From the winners of each event, the State Championship bracket will be made. Please refer to the Rules and Regulations page before attendence.
